01 · About Rhysil Forensics
Where Science
Becomes the Witness
Rhysil Forensics is a forensic criminal investigations firm under Beyond Limits Rhysil Enterprise Company, headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We exist to bridge the gap between what the eye claims to see and what science confirms.

"To bring scientific transformation to criminal investigations — moving beyond perception and assumption, into the undeniable language of forensic evidence. We give science a voice so that justice may be served."

Our Purpose

Every crime scene holds a truth. Every piece of evidence carries a story. Rhysil Forensics exists to read those stories with scientific precision and deliver them, unaltered and undeniable, to those who serve justice.

Who We Serve

We serve Kenya's law enforcement agencies, courts, legal professionals, and government bodies — any institution committed to truth and accountability in criminal justice.

Our Commitment

Reliability and trustworthiness are not marketing words at Rhysil — they are operational requirements. Every case we handle is treated with the same standard: the full weight of scientific truth.

02 · Services
Full-Spectrum
Forensic Services
Rhysil Forensics delivers an integrated suite of forensic and criminal investigation services across all major disciplines — from the first fingerprint lifted at a scene to the expert testimony delivered in court.
Flagship
Biometric · Core Specialty
Fingerprint Analysis (Dactyloscopy)
Latent, patent & plastic print recovery, AFIS database comparison, minutiae mapping, and ridge detail analysis. The science of identity through touch.
Biological
DNA & Serology
Biological evidence collection, DNA profiling and comparison, blood pattern analysis, and species identification from crime scenes.
Digital
Digital Forensics
Device recovery and imaging, deleted data extraction, metadata analysis, and maintaining cyber-evidence chain of custody.
Scene Investigation
Crime Scene Investigation
Full scene documentation, systematic evidence collection, 3D reconstruction, and scene mapping with photographic record.
Medical
Forensic Pathology Support
Cause-of-death investigation coordination, autopsy report analysis, wound pattern interpretation, and time-of-death estimation.
Document Examination
Questioned Documents
Handwriting analysis, forgery and alteration detection, ink and paper examination, and signature verification.
Toxicology
Forensic Toxicology
Substance identification from biological samples, poisoning case analysis, drug and alcohol quantification.
Legal
Expert Witness Testimony
Court-ready forensic reports, professional expert witness services, and evidence presentation for legal proceedings.
Ballistics
Firearms & Ballistic Analysis
Tool mark and striation examination, gunshot residue analysis, bullet trajectory mapping, and firearm identification.
03 · The Rhysil Position on Justice

Compromise.

The Law does not compromise.
II.

At Rhysil Forensics, we don't interpret evidence to fit a narrative — we let evidence build the narrative.
